FAQ

Answers to the common questions.

Still unsure? Download PillRem free and explore risk-free — no account required to get started.

What is the best medication reminder app for iPhone? +
PillRem is a calm, private medication reminder app designed specifically for iPhone. It offers smart pill reminders, adherence streaks, health metric tracking, family profiles and doctor-ready PDF reports — all without ads or selling your data. Its clean "Calm Apothecary" design makes it especially popular with people managing chronic conditions and caregivers helping elderly parents.
How does a pill reminder app work? +
A pill reminder app like PillRem lets you add your medications, set a schedule and receive local notifications at exactly the right times. You tap to mark each dose as taken, and the app keeps a running log of your adherence that you can review or share with your doctor. PillRem also tracks refill timing so you don't run out of prescriptions.
Can I use PillRem to manage medications for my elderly parents? +
Yes — PillRem is built for caregiver medication management. Create a separate profile for each person (yourself, a parent, a child, even a pet) and PillRem handles each schedule independently. Many of our most loyal users are adult children tracking medications for elderly parents who live in a different home.
Is my health data safe with PillRem? +
Your medication data stays on your device by default. PillRem does not sell your health data, does not run ads, and uses local iOS notifications for reminders — so you can track sensitive medications privately without anything being uploaded to a server you don't control.
Does PillRem work without internet? +
Yes. Core features — reminders, medication logs, adherence tracking and PDF report generation — all work fully offline on your iPhone. Optional iCloud sync is available if you want to back up your data across devices.
Can I share medication reports with my doctor? +
Yes. PillRem generates doctor-ready PDF reports in one tap that include your adherence history, recent doses, and any health metrics you've logged. Share them by email, message, AirDrop, or print them for in-person appointments.
Is PillRem free? +
PillRem is free to download from the App Store and the core medication reminder features are free to use. Optional premium features — like unlimited family profiles and extended history — are available via an affordable subscription.
What makes PillRem different from other pill reminder apps? +
Three things: a calm, intentional design that treats you like a person and not a patient; strong privacy (no ads, no tracking); and deep features like doctor-ready PDF reports and multi-profile support that go beyond basic reminders. PillRem is what a premium medication tracker should feel like.
